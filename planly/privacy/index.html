Planly – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

Summary

Planly is designed to respect your privacy. This page is a simple, human-readable overview. For App Store review, you can also generate a formal policy using your preferred generator and replace this content.

Data collection

What Planly collects

Planly primarily stores your tasks and settings on your device and, if enabled, in iCloud so they can sync across your Apple devices. Planly does not create its own account system.

Planly does not:

  • Sell your data.
  • Track you across other apps or websites.
  • Use third‑party advertising SDKs.
iCloud

iCloud and Apple services

If you enable iCloud for Planly, your data is stored in your personal iCloud account under Apple’s terms and encryption. Apple may process basic analytics related to your iCloud account and device as described in Apple’s privacy policy.

Support

Support email

When you email support at fosterappshelp+planly@gmail.com, the contents of your email are stored in your email provider’s systems. Please avoid including sensitive personal information in support requests.

Changes

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated as Planly evolves. When it changes, the “Last updated” date at the top of the page will be revised. Significant changes may also be mentioned in release notes.

Contact

Questions
