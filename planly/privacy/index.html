Planly – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Planly (“Planly,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) handles information when you use the Planly mobile application (the “App”). Planly is designed to be private by default. We do not run third‑party ads or analytics, and we do not sell your personal information.

Information we collect

Information you provide

Planly does not require an account. You may enter content such as tasks, reflections, and settings. This content is stored on your device. If you enable iCloud sync, that content is stored in your personal iCloud account so it can sync across your Apple devices.

Support communications

If you contact support at fosterappshelp+planly@gmail.com, we will receive the information you choose to share in your message and any attachments you provide.

In‑app purchases

If you purchase a subscription or other in‑app item, the transaction is processed by the app store provider (Apple App Store, and Google Play if applicable). We do not receive your payment card details. The app store may provide us with purchase status information (for example, whether a subscription is active).

Information we do not collect

Planly does not collect:

  • Location data (GPS).
  • Contacts, photos, or phonebook data.
  • Cross‑app or cross‑website tracking data.
  • Advertising identifiers or third‑party analytics data.
How we use information

We use information only to provide and improve the App, including:

  • Storing your tasks and settings on your device.
  • Syncing your data via iCloud if you enable it.
  • Responding to support requests you send us.
  • Verifying purchase status for premium features.
Sharing and disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We share information only in the following limited cases:

  • With Apple (and Google Play if applicable) to process purchases and manage subscriptions.
  • With Apple iCloud when you enable iCloud sync; your data is stored in your personal iCloud account under Apple’s terms.
  • If required to comply with applicable law or a valid legal process.
Notifications

Local notifications

If you enable reminders, Planly schedules notifications locally on your device. Notification settings and times are stored on your device.

Data retention

Planly keeps your content on your device until you delete it or remove the App. If you enable iCloud sync, your data remains in iCloud until you delete it from the App or from iCloud storage.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information stored within the App and when using iCloud. However, no method of storage or transmission is 100% secure.

Children’s privacy

Planly is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will delete it.

Your choices

You can disable iCloud sync in the App settings, delete content within the App, or remove the App to delete local data. If you contact support, you can request that we delete the message thread where feasible.

International users

Planly is operated from the United States. If you use the App from other regions, your information may be processed in the United States or where Apple provides iCloud services. By using the App, you consent to this processing.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top of this page will reflect the latest version.

Contact

Questions

If you have any questions about this policy or how Planly handles data, contact:
