Stead – Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

Summary

Stead is designed to respect your privacy. Your home details, maintenance tasks, and service records stay on your device and, if you choose, in your personal iCloud account. Stead does not run its own servers or create user accounts.

Data collection

What Stead collects

Stead is developed and operated by Foster Apps LLC. Stead stores your home information, maintenance tasks, seasonal checklists, and service history on your device and, if enabled, in iCloud so they sync across your Apple devices. Stead does not create its own account system.

Stead does not:

  • Sell your data.
  • Track you across other apps or websites.
  • Use third‑party advertising SDKs.
  • Share your home or personal information with any third party.
iCloud

iCloud and Apple services

If you enable iCloud for Stead, your data is stored in your personal iCloud account under Apple's terms and encryption. Apple may process basic analytics related to your iCloud account and device as described in Apple's privacy policy.

Notifications

Push notifications

Stead can send local maintenance reminders. These are scheduled entirely on your device — no notification data is sent to external servers. You can manage or disable notifications at any time in iOS Settings.

Subscriptions

In-app purchases

Stead Pro is available as a subscription through Apple's App Store. All billing is handled by Apple. Stead never sees your payment details. Subscription management and cancellation are handled through your Apple ID settings.

Support

Support email

When you email support at support+stead@fosterapps.com, the contents of your email are stored in your email provider's systems. Please avoid including sensitive personal information in support requests.

Changes

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated as Stead evolves. When it changes, the "Last updated" date at the top of the page will be revised. Significant changes may also be mentioned in release notes.

Contact

Questions

If you have any questions about this policy or how Stead handles data, contact:
