What the App does

Duct sizing estimates

The App estimates round and rectangular duct sizes, equivalent diameters, and friction rates using standard published equal-friction sizing methods (commonly referenced SMACNA/ASHRAE residential and light-commercial duct sizing guidance) and the Altshul-Tsal approximation of the Colebrook-White friction equation. It also provides general reference information (typical airflow ranges, typical target velocities) drawn from the same class of published references.
