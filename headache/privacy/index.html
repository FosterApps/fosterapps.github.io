Headache Journal – Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 23, 2026

Summary

Headache Journal is designed to respect your privacy. Your headache logs, trigger history, and medication records stay on your device and, if you choose, in your personal iCloud account. Headache Journal does not run its own servers or create user accounts.

Data collection

What Headache Journal collects

Headache Journal is developed and operated by Foster Apps LLC. The app stores your headache entries, trigger tags, symptom notes, and medication logs on your device and, if enabled, in iCloud so they sync across your Apple devices.

Headache Journal does not:

  • Sell your health data.
  • Track you across other apps or websites.
  • Use third-party advertising SDKs.
  • Share your personal or health information with any third party.
  • Require an account or email address.
iCloud

iCloud backup

If you enable iCloud backup in Headache Journal Pro, your headache data is stored in your personal iCloud account under Apple's terms and encryption. Foster Apps cannot access this data. Apple may process basic analytics related to your iCloud account and device as described in Apple's privacy policy.

Notifications

Push notifications

Headache Journal can send local reminders to log headaches. These are scheduled entirely on your device — no notification data is sent to external servers. You can manage or disable notifications at any time in iOS Settings.

Subscriptions

In-app purchases

Headache Journal Pro is available as a subscription through Apple's App Store. All billing is handled by Apple. Headache Journal never sees your payment details. Subscription management and cancellation are handled through your Apple ID settings.

PDF Export

Doctor reports

When you export a PDF report, it is generated entirely on your device. Headache Journal does not upload your data to any server to produce the report. Sharing is handled by iOS's system share sheet — the data goes only to the destination you choose.

Support

Support email

When you email support at support+headache@fosterapps.com, the contents of your email are stored in your email provider's systems. Please avoid including sensitive health information in support requests.

Changes

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated as Headache Journal evolves. When it changes, the "Last updated" date at the top of the page will be revised. Significant changes may also be mentioned in release notes.

Contact

Questions

If you have any questions about this policy or how Headache Journal handles your data, contact:
