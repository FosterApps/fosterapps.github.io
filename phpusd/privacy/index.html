PHP to USD Conversion – Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 12, 2026

Summary

PHP to USD Conversion is designed to respect your privacy. Your saved amounts and rate alert settings stay on your device. The app does not require an account, does not use advertising, and does not sell or share your personal data.

Data collection

What PHP to USD Conversion collects

PHP to USD Conversion is developed and operated by Foster Apps LLC. The app stores your saved conversion amounts, custom names, and rate alert thresholds locally on your device. This data never leaves your device.

PHP to USD Conversion does not:

  • Collect or store your name, email address, or any personally identifying information
  • Require an account or login of any kind
  • Use advertising identifiers or build advertising profiles
  • Sell your data to third parties
Anonymous usage analytics

Product analytics

The app records a small set of anonymous, aggregate usage events (such as first open, reaching a core feature, and purchase completion) to help us understand which parts of the app are useful. Each installation is identified only by a random ID generated on your device — not your Apple ID, name, or any other personal identifier — and Apple's App Privacy label for this app reflects this as "Product Interaction, not linked to identity." No saved amounts, rate thresholds, or other content you enter is ever included in these events.

Notifications

Rate alert notifications

If you set a rate alert, PHP to USD Conversion requests permission to send you a local notification when your target rate is reached. This permission is requested only when you choose to set your first alert — never at launch. You can revoke notification access at any time in iOS Settings → Notifications.

Purchases

In-App Purchases

PHP to USD Conversion offers an optional Pro subscription processed entirely through Apple's App Store (StoreKit). Foster Apps does not receive or store your payment information. Purchase history is managed by Apple. See Apple's privacy policy at apple.com/legal/privacy.

Crash Reporting

Diagnostics

If you have enabled "Share with App Developers" in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Analytics & Improvements, Apple may provide anonymised crash reports to Foster Apps. These reports contain no personally identifying information and are used solely to fix bugs.

Third-party services

Exchange rate data

PHP to USD Conversion fetches live exchange rate data from a third-party currency rate provider to display accurate conversions. This request does not include any personal information — only the currency pair being converted.

Children

Children's privacy

PHP to USD Conversion does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app contains no advertising, no account creation, and no social features.

Contact

Questions

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at privacy@fosterapps.com.
